Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring on the rafting trip?

We recommend a swimsuit, quick-drying clothes, water shoes or sandals with a heel strap, sunscreen, a hat, and a water bottle.

Do I need prior rafting experience?

No experience is necessary! Our guides provide all instructions and ensure a safe, enjoyable trip for all skill levels.

Is it safe for kids?

Yes, we offer family-friendly trips suitable for children aged 8 and up. Life jackets and safety gear are provided for everyone.

What if the weather is bad?

Trips run rain or shine. We only cancel or reschedule for severe weather or unsafe river conditions.

How long are the trips?

Trips range from half-day to multi-day adventures. Exact duration depends on the package you choose.

How far in advance should I book my trip?

If you have specific dates you need, we recommend booking a year in advance. If you can be more flexible 6 months in advance is usually sufficient.
